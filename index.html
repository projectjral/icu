Quezon City Delos Santos ( Anor, Tolentino )

The Delos Santos section in Quezon City is known for its vibrant community and diverse amenities. From bustling marketplaces to serene parks, this area offers a blend of urban convenience and suburban tranquility.

With its strategic location and accessibility to key establishments, it's a popular choice for both residents and visitors alike. Whether you're looking for a place to unwind or explore, the Delos Santos section has something to offer for everyone.

The administration of the Delos Santos section

Quezon City is typically overseen by the local government, particularly the barangay (neighborhood) council. The barangay council plays a crucial role in managing the day-to-day affairs of the community, including sanitation, public safety, and community development initiatives.

The Barangay Captain, elected by the residents, leads the barangay council. They act as the chief executive officer of the barangay, overseeing its operations and representing its interests in dealings with higher levels of government. Additionally, the council consists of elected barangay kagawads (councilors) who assist in decision-making and policy implementation.

ogether, the barangay council works to address the needs and concerns of residents, organize community events, manage local infrastructure projects, and ensure the overall welfare of the Delos Santos section. They also collaborate with city authorities on broader issues affecting the area, such as zoning regulations, public services, and emergency response.
